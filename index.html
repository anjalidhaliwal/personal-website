Anjali Eras Over The Years

Age 9-15: Rainbow Loom Projects

Started with DIY projects like making purses and bracelets with rainbow loom, selling them at local markets, and donating the profits to charity.

Age 8-21: Singing & Musical Theatre Era

Started a YouTube channel at age 8, singing covers and original songs, and performed in a few musical theatre productions, including lead roles in high school.

Age 13-15: Biotech Era

Worked at a few biotech labs growing up!

Age 16-17: Nutri Lipbalms Era

Co-created a line of organic lip balms, sold 300 units at local markets and online, donating 100% of profits to local charities.

Age 16-21: Youth Helping Youth Era

Scaled to 7 chapters across North America, 200+ volunteers, 30K students helped, 9M impressions, $100K+ in government grants awarded. Became the fastest-growing youth-led NPO in Canada 2020 and 2021.

Age 19-21: Influencer & Motivational Speaker Era

Worked with many big tech brands, and spoke over 500 events! Was kinda crazy to fly all over the world as a 20 something to speak to massive crowds of people

Age 19-23: Finance Era

Worked at many corporate finance companies (Nasdaq, Canadian Pension Plan Investment Board, Inovia Capital, Front Row Ventures, N+1 Analytics Hedge Fund).

Age 20-21: Digital Nomad Era

Traveled to 10+ countries, worked and lived in many different cities. I was in Dubai working under Nas Daily, Singapore working at a Hawker store for a day, Vietnam working at a crypto hedge fund and so many other crazy experiences!

Age 22-23: Avo Era

Co-founded Avo from concept to a venture-backed crypto marketplace processing more than $14 million in total transaction value, compounding 40 percent month-over-month, and serving over 8,000 active traders

Age 23-Present: Builder Era

More coming soon! But, I promise to build in public!
